Description

Attention Chasseur!


Do you have the potential to come up with logical-driven codes under the time constraint? or are you looking for a platform to showcase your talent?
If yes, the Xtrofest 3.0 presents to you a platform where people with fervour of codes assemble and compete in two levels. A Riveting competitive programming event 'Arcadeous'. The event not only offers the participants a set of insightful questions but also allows them to think out of the box giving them a thrilling competitive environment with exciting prizes.

PLATFORM: Hackerearth / Hackerrank

It is an individual event.


Rounds

LEVEL 1:

You will be given three coding questions (Easy, Medium, Difficult) and should code them within a time span of 120 minutes. If the student successfully clears the cut off, he/she will be promoted to the next level of Arcade Geeks.

LEVEL2:

You will be given a gaming problem which will consist of two modules and your job is to code the game so that it reaches its desired destination(output) within 180 minutes.

Rules


  • This is an Individual Coding Competition for all students across India.

  • Please refrain from discussing strategy during the contest.

  • Each challenge has a pre-determined score.

  • All submissions are run through a plagiarism detector. Any case of code plagiarism will disqualify both users from the contest.

  • Participants are ranked by score, with the cumulative time taken (between the contest's start time and the time of your correct submission) used to break ties.

  • Top three coders from different college will be announced winners.


Prizes

Arcadeous brings huge prizes.
Cash Prizes worth INR 3,000 for first prize
INR 2,000 for second prize
INR 1,000 for third prize winners
