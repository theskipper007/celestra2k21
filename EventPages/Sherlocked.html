Description

The Fun is Real. The Adventure is real. The presumption is real.

Here comes the ultimate ruminating experience by placing individuals in a fun, high-pressure environment where they must rack one’s brain to succeed, the result is a cohesive person who works more effectively. The soap opera hunt is a witful and interesting game based on your favourite series. If you are a series addict here's your challenge: hop, jump in and use all of your series knowledge to escape the room and win interesting prizes. Participants HAVE to think exemplarily to hunt the room and slowly unravel the series of challenges. There will be three different levels with unique themes. The participant who comes out first will be the winner.
