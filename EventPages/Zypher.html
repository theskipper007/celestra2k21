Rounds

As an offshoot of this hackathon, we are encouraging participants to submit their ideas for the following three rounds:
Participants can register and make their submissions. These submissions must contain solutions to the problem statements that the participants selected while registering.

Round 1: Prototype -Participants have to submit their prototype model using any designing software like adobe or figma.

Round 2: Implementation of front end (Web/Mobile)- Shortlisted participants from the round 1 are invited to present their implementation of front end based on the ideas that were proposed during the prototype round.

Round 3: Overall Implementation - Shortlisted participants from the round 2 are invited to present their overall implementation of front end/back end based on the ideas that were proposed during the first two rounds.

Rules

  • 1.This is an Individual/Team participation Hackathon.

  • 2.A team can have maximum of 2 members.

  • 3.You must submit your idea that is in alignment with one of the given problem statements.

  • 4.This Hackathon will consist of 3 phases Registration, Abstract submission and Shortlisted participants will be able to present their idea and prototype. The last date to submit your abstract is on 19th September, 2021 11.59 PM IST.

  • 5.You are expected to come up with new and innovative ideas. Please note that any idea that has been copied or borrowed from any source including but not limited to internet sources may not be considered for the presentation round.

  • 6.Those who are judged by the management to have committed acts of vandalism or violations of public order and morals at symposium related events or on hackathon related websites/chat group will be disqualified from participation.
