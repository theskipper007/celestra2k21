About


Celestra, Xtrofest 3.0 is a national level virtual symposium conducted by Sri Sai Ram Engineering College, Chennai. The symposium aims in developing entrepreneurial skills while exploring their technical skills and enhancing their conceptual knowledge and coming up with creative and unique solutions. This is a 2 days event that will help young minds showcase their talents and expertise. It will keep you immersed in various competitions and skillful activities in a wider spectrum. Embark yourself in a fun and learning experience with Celestra!!
